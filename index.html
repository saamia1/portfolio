Interactive Comic

This team project was an interactive comic titled "Tux Saves the Day… or Not." The story follows Tux, a superhero penguin, faced with a moral dilemma at an overcrowded penguin pond. A hungry Baby Penguin ventures into a bear’s territory in search of food, triggering a tense standoff. The reader is invited to decide whether Tux intervenes to help or walks away — shaping the outcome of the story. Through simple animations and branching panels, the comic explores themes of empathy, power dynamics, and survival. I contributed to both story development and implementation, and learned how to balance narrative with interactivity.

Sound Project

This team project explored storytelling through sound, using only audio cues to build atmosphere and tension. Our story follows a girl alone in her home, studying peacefully when she’s startled by the sound of a bottle falling in the kitchen. As she cautiously searches the house—calling for her mom and hearing only silence—the suspense builds, until she finally discovers the source: her playful cat. We leaned into a spooky theme for this piece, which was a creative challenge and a genre shift from our usual work. Crafting the emotions purely through ambient sound and pacing was a uniquely immersive experience.
